About Me

Hello to all that view my portfolio!! I am always looking for new ways to expand and fine-tune my practice. Since childhood, I've had a fascination with constructing and deconstructing things. I'm sure that seems vague, but that's becasue it applies to almost everything I come across. How does it work? Could it be better? What happens if I do this? These are just a few common questions that ignite my mind and kickstart a series (containing at least 3 seasons with a minimum of 12 episodes per season) of trial and error runs.
